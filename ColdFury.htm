Cold Fury - Premium German Lager

This is our first Lager - following the classic German style and using the world famous Hallertau hops from Bavaria

Profile - Cold Fury - Premium German Lager

ABV: 5.2%

IBU: 35

Malts: Lager, Munich and Vienna

Hops: Magnum, Hallertau Tradition, Hallertau Mittelfrau, Hallertau Hersbrucker, Hallertau Blanc, Liberty

Yeast: Fermentis W34/70

Water: Soft

Colour: Bright gold. Creamy white head.

Smell: Powerful hop aroma

Taste: Clean, bready aromas lead to a full-bodied malt flavour with an assertive bitterness. Medium carbonation allows for a creamy, textured mouthfeel, balanced with a floral, subtle citrus hop character and dry lingering bitter finish.

Brew notes - Cold Fury - Premium German Lager

Inspired by the Bavarian region of Hallertau; arguably the largest continual hop growing area in the world. In this lager we use six hop varieties, from both the old and new world, but all with connections to the Hallertau region giving a distinctive flavour and lingering, refreshing bitterness.
