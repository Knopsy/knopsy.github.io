Revolver I - West Coast IPA

This is our first release of our specialist India Pale Ale series where we showcase specific hops and styles of IPA from around the world.

Profile - Revolver I - West Coast IPA

ABV: 6%

IBU: 50

Malts: Pale Ale, Caramalt, Munich and Torrified Wheat

Hops: Magnum, Summit, Chinook and Centennial

Water: Hard

Colour: Deep Golden

Smell: Powerful hop aroma

Taste: Strong upfront bitterness. Juicy tangerine and grapefruit flavours from Summit, Chinook and Centennial hops balance with a full honey malt sweetness. Strong pine flavour and hop character dominate the lingering bitter finish.

Brew notes - Revolver I - West Coast IPA

This beer is a celebration of the classic West Coast IPA style which helped to define the American craft beer movement. A full and rich malt body acts as the stage for three American hop varieties which bring huge fruit flavours and a big, brash bitterness.
