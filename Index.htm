Knops Brewing Company

We are a modern brewery; refining old and new styles and creating new ones.

Our brewing is based on science, skill and equipment.

We understand the ingredients, how to combine them and, more importantly, how to consistently brew beer that always tastes great.

We believe in sharing our knowledge and experience whenever possible as greater understanding leads to greater enjoyment.
